# Responsible AI Notes

## Design principles

- Use synthetic or approved data only.
- Keep humans responsible for final decisions.
- Avoid destructive actions without confirmation.
- Log decisions and uncertainty.
- Separate retrieval, reasoning, and output generation.
- Evaluate outputs before operational use.

## Failure cases to test

- Missing context
- Ambiguous request
- Incorrect classification
- Unsupported legal/policy question
- Hallucinated source
- Overconfident recommendation
